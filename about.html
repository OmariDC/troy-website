Hi, I’m Troy

Looking beneath the surface.

My work centres on the hidden layers of human experience: the thoughts, emotions and relational patterns that can remain unspoken.

I work with individuals, couples and groups to explore what sits beneath the surface, make sense of complex feelings and create space for meaningful personal and relational change.

I bring sensitivity, humour when appropriate, integrity and authenticity to the work.

A little about me

Experience shapes how we see.

I am an 80s baby who was educated in both England and America. My working life has included a variety of roles that eventually led me towards a vocation I am proud to have found.

I enjoy the gym and anime. Being diagnosed later in life with autism and ADHD—AuDHD—has also shaped my understanding of what it can mean to experience the world differently.
