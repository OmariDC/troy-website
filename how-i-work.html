How I work

A space we create together.

Therapy is not something done to you. The relationship we create becomes part of the work.

Relational work

Invite the difficult things into the room.

I aim to offer consistency, honesty and enough safety for the thoughts, feelings and behaviours that may bring fear into the room.

Rather than pushing them away, we can invite them into the conversation and explore what a different outcome might feel like.

How we relate and what we co-create can offer opportunities for deep reflection, growth and change.

What you can expect

Warmth. Honesty. Depth.

  • Sensitivity
  • Humour when appropriate
  • Integrity and authenticity
  • Commitment to the themes you bring
  • Space for reflection, challenge and change

Long- or short-term work.

Some people come with one clear concern. Others need time to understand patterns developed across years and relationships.

The length and focus of our work can be discussed together. Sessions may be offered for 60 or 90 minutes, with some sessions for clients under 18 lasting 45–60 minutes.
